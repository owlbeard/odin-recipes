Cream&Soy Chicken

This recipe will make everyone in the household (or whomever you are serving ) drool outta their goddamn minds! Totally easy to prepare, my Cream&Soy Chicken recipe will leave everyone wanting more. After trying this recipe you won't want to make your chickens any other way.

Ingredients:

  • 800 gram boneless and skinless chicken thigh
  • 200 ml heavy cream
  • 5 tbsp salt-reduced soy sauce
  • cooking oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • black pepper and salt to taste
  • nutmeg, thinly chopped parsley (optional but not really)

Preparation:

  1. Put some cooking oil in the pan (preferably a non-stick one) and heat it for 15 seconds.
  2. After the pan is nicely heated, put your diced onions in and roast them until they are caramelized.
  3. Then, add your diced garlic and continue roasting them for 1 minute.
  4. You can now add your chicken (whole or in medium sized chunks) and start searing them.
  5. Cook the chicken until it reabsorbs the juices it has released.
  6. Add 5 table spoons of soy sauce and mix it with the chicken.
  7. Add 200 mls of heavy cream and let it cook for 5 more minutes.
  8. Season it with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  9. Sprinkle the thinly chopped parsley and mix the seasoning/garnish with the meal and it's ready to be served!
  10. Dig in!
